Boldspark

boldspark.co

Boldspark is where I really felt like I was hitting my stride with what I wanted a website to convey. I wanted to go all in on the "StoryBrand" framework and use that in my copy on every section. It's also heavily influenced by my dabbling in Webflow.

I came up with the name because I wanted to give a nod to my old man, who's an entrepreneur and master electrician for over 30 years.
